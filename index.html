The Venue

The pride of Thiruvalla

Yahir Auditorium is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure spread over three floors to host your events at their finest. Centralized air conditioning serves every floor, while echo-proof interiors and high-end technical support raise every occasion to a higher standard.

With live telecast of your function, we bring your memorable event even closer to your loved ones. Each hall accommodates a thousand guests — and simultaneous events can be hosted on prior request.
